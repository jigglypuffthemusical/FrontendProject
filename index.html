About

Chelsea's bakery opened it's doors on May 2017 and has been famous for it's delicous pastries and amazing wedding cakes. We are excited to introduce our new head pastry chef Chelsea Mofiz. She has experience with celebrity chef Nathan Lyon as well as crew of Carlos Bakery from the hit show Cake Boss. She is a local graduate from Sullivan University in Baking and Pastry Arts and has work experience from Germany. Join us daily for fresh brewed coffee and warm pastries to start off your morning.
